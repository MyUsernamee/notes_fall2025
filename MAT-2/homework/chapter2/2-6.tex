In Problems 1–6 solve the given Bernoulli equation.

\subparagraph{1}

\[
x \frac{dy}{dx} + y = \frac{1}{y^2}
\]
\vfill



\subparagraph{3}

\[
\frac{dy}{dx} = y(xy^3 - 1)
\]
\vfill


\pagebreak


\subparagraph{5}

\[
x^2 \frac{dy}{dx} + y^2 = xy
\]
\vfill

In Problems 7–10 solve the given differential equation subject to the indicated initial condition.

\subparagraph{7}

\[
x^2 \frac{dy}{dx} - 2xy = 3y^4, y(1) = \frac{1}{2}
\]
\vfill


\pagebreak


\subparagraph{9}

\[
xy(1 + xy^2) \frac{dy}{dx} = 1, y(1) = 0
\]
\vfill


\pagebreak
\subsection*{Answers:}


\subparagraph{1}

\[
y^3 = 1 + cx^{-3}
\]
\vfill



\subparagraph{3}

\[
y^{-3} = x + \frac{1}{3} + ce^{3x}
\]
\vfill



\subparagraph{5}

\[
e^{x/y} = cx
\]
\vfill



\subparagraph{7}

\[
y^{-3} = -\frac{9}{5}x^{-1} + \frac{59}{5}x^{-6}
\]
\vfill



\subparagraph{9}

\[
x^{-1} = 2 - y^2 - e^{-y^2/2}, the equation is Bernoulli in the variable x.
\]
\vfill
